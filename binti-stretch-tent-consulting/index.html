Common Questions

How much should I invest in my first tent?

The answer depends on your target market, event size and business goals. This is covered during consultation.

Should I import or buy locally?

Both options have advantages. Virginia helps evaluate quality, cost, shipping and long-term value.

What tent size should I start with?

This depends on the market you intend to serve and your available budget.

Do I need trained riggers?

Proper setup is essential for safety, efficiency and protecting your investment.

What accessories are required?

Poles, pegs, ratchets, ropes, gutters and maintenance equipment are critical components of a professional setup.
